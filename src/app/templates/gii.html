GII vs % Urban Population:

This plot shows the relationship of the Gender Inequality Index and % of urban population of a country for a given time period. We can see that the countries that are more urbanized have less disparity between male and female inhabitants.




GII vs GDP

This plot shows the relationship between Gender Inequality and Gross Domestic product. It is mostly consistent, with Gender Inequality dropping as GDP rises. A notable outlier is Qatar, which has seen its GDP drop by roughly 20% in since 2008, but has retained relatively high gender inequality, despite its GDP, which is still quite high.

GII vs % population with safe water:

This plot shows the relationship of the Gender Inequality Index and % population with access to safe water of a country. We see countries with better access to safe drinking water have greater equality between males and females.
